# Mapping Elf Market Research Analysis

## Trigger Criteria

Use this skill for market research, competitor analysis, review mining, SEO keyword analysis, user segmentation, feature demand validation, and structured research planning.

Do not use this skill to directly change product code. Hand implementation ideas to `mapping-elf-sub-agent-coordinator`.

## Primary Ownership

- Research plans and analysis reports
- Data-source inventory and collection strategy
- Cleaning, coding, metric, and segmentation definitions
- Product insight handoff for Mapping Elf roadmap or experiments

## Required First Reads

- Read `README.md` to understand current product scope.
- Read `references/research-workflow.md` before designing data collection or analysis.
- If using external sources, verify current source availability and collection constraints before presenting them as facts.

## Gotchas

- Separate observed data from hypotheses and product intuition.
- Do not assume access to paid tools, private APIs, or credentials.
- Respect source terms, privacy, and rate limits.
- Market insight must be specific enough to hand off as a feature, experiment, positioning, or validation task.
- For time-sensitive market facts, verify current data before answering.

## Handoff Format

Provide research question, source list, collection method, sample/filters, metrics, analysis method, confidence limits, and product implications.

## Verification Checklist

- Label data-backed findings versus assumptions.
- Include date range and source constraints.
- If a product change is recommended, hand it off with target user, success metric, and suggested owner skill.
